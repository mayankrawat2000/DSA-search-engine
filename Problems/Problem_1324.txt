There are nn customers in the cafeteria. Each of them wants to buy a hamburger. The ii-th customer has aiai coins, and they will buy a hamburger if it costs at most aiai coins.
Suppose the cost of the hamburger is mm. Then the number of coins the cafeteria earns is mm multiplied by the number of people who buy a hamburger if it costs mm. Your task is to calculate the maximum number of coins the cafeteria can earn.
